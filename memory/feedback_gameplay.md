---
name: Feedback - Gameplay Design Preferences
description: User's gameplay preferences — alive world, no random panic, Enter to exit ship, realistic behaviors
type: feedback
---

- Humans should only panic when actively attacked (beam, flamethrower, high terror), NOT when ship flies overhead.
- Exit ship key = Enter (not X). Space = jump when on foot (no beam).
- Camera must follow alien when on foot, not the ship.
- Caves should be invisible from the surface — only render when player is inside.
- People/animals in water should drown and sink. Nothing should spawn in water.
- World should feel alive: farmers farm, commuters commute, monkeys eat bananas from trees, joggers jog.
- Each biome should have its own population type (city people in city, indigenous in jungle, etc.).
- Don't overcrowd — spread things out, give buildings space.
- Mountains should be large with big explorable caves you walk through on foot.
- The after-effects of explosions matter — fire and smoke should look good, not just circles.
**Why:** User wants an immersive world you enjoy just flying around and observing.
**How to apply:** When adding features, always think about how it affects the "alive world" feel.
